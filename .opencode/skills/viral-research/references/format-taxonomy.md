# Format Taxonomy

Classification system for faceless Instagram Reels. Every Reel analyzed should be assigned
one tier and (if premium) one sub-format. These tiers were derived from frame-by-frame analysis
of 10 Reels across sobriety, wellness, and premium brand niches.

## Niche Tiers (T1-T4)

These are the formats commonly found within specific niches. They require minimal production
investment and are optimized for raw reach and engagement.

### T1: Meme Reel

- **Example:** @yoursoberpal — kayaker in rapids + "Sobriety is a beautiful journey"
- **Structure:** Static text overlay on dynamic footage that visually contradicts the text
- **Viral mechanism:** Humor through contrast. The text sets an expectation; the footage shatters it
- **Production cost:** ~5 minutes. Stock footage + 2 text overlays
- **Duration sweet spot:** 5-8 seconds
- **Best for:** Shareability, DM sends, reaching new audiences beyond your followers
- **Engagement profile:** Very high likes-per-second ratio. High share rate. Low save rate.
- **Identifying signals:** Short duration, comedic footage, static text that contradicts the visual

### T2: Quote Card Reel

- **Example:** @wisclandscaping — AI sunset beach + "I would've been 2 years sober today"
- **Structure:** Single motivational/regret/urgency quote on a static or near-static background image
- **Viral mechanism:** Regret/urgency (FOMO), loss aversion, "I need to share this" impulse
- **Production cost:** ~5 minutes. AI-generated or stock image + bold centered text
- **Duration sweet spot:** 5-7 seconds
- **Best for:** Raw reach, saves, shares. Highest engagement ceiling in our dataset (47.6K likes)
- **Engagement profile:** Highest likes-per-second of any format. Very high save rate.
- **Identifying signals:** Static image (often AI-generated), bold stacked text, no animation
- **⚠️ Motion requirement:** Never publish as a static image. Always render with text animation (fade-in, typewriter, or staggered reveal) + slow background motion (Ken Burns zoom/pan, parallax, or subtle particle effect). Duration 5-7s. Instagram's algorithm weights watch time and completion rate — static images as Reels get deprioritized vs actual video content.

### T3: Text Card Reel

- **Example:** @soberflourish — sunset timelapse + myth vs reality comparison list
- **Structure:** Two-section text card (myth list vs reality list, or before/after) on subtle background motion
- **Viral mechanism:** Visual asymmetry. Short dismissive myth list vs long aspirational reality list
- **Production cost:** ~15 minutes. Timelapse or stock video + layered text
- **Duration sweet spot:** 6-10 seconds
- **Best for:** Saves, identity validation, building niche authority
- **Engagement profile:** High save rate. Moderate likes. Strong in-niche resonance.
- **Identifying signals:** Two distinct text sections, comparison structure, subtle background motion

### T4: Truth Bomb Reel

- **Example:** @themagicisinthemiddle — sunset timelapse + three-part logical argument
- **Structure:** Multi-part text making a provocative argument (fact → irony → conclusion)
- **Viral mechanism:** Outrage + validation. Articulates something the audience feels but can't express
- **Production cost:** ~15 minutes. Real footage + clean typography
- **Duration sweet spot:** 7-10 seconds
- **Best for:** Comments, debate, shares. Builds authority and trust
- **Engagement profile:** High comment rate. Moderate likes. Strong share-to-save ratio.
- **Identifying signals:** Three-part argument structure, clean serif typography, contemplative footage

## Premium Sub-Formats (P1-P3)

These are formats used by established brands (Headspace, Calm, WHOOP, Oura). They require
higher production investment but create stronger brand differentiation. They're replicable
using tools like Remotion (programmatic video) and fal.ai (AI image/video generation).

### P1: Functional Loop

- **Examples:** Headspace breathing globe, Calm breathing circle
- **Structure:** Animated interactive element (breathing guide, meditation timer) that loops seamlessly
- **Viral mechanism:** Content IS the product experience. Viewer participates (breathes along).
  Seamless loop = infinite watch time = algorithm boost
- **Production cost:** 4-8 hours (initial template). Variations are fast once template exists.
- **Key production elements:**
  - Custom illustration or motion graphics (not stock)
  - Seamless loop (last frame matches first frame)
  - Minimal text (1-2 words: "Inhale," "Exhale")
  - Monochromatic brand palette
  - Easing curves on all animations
- **Remotion feasibility:** HIGH — circle/shape animation, text fade, particle systems
- **Identifying signals:** Looping animation, minimal text, brand mascot/illustration, monochromatic

### P2: Data Viz Humor

- **Examples:** WHOOP brain destruction at low sleep %, WHOOP heartbeat challenge
- **Structure:** Animated data metric (gauge, counter, graph) paired with a visual metaphor or
  gamification hook
- **Viral mechanism:** Relatable data + humor (brain exploding at 4% sleep), or interactive challenge
  ("double-tap to match the heart rate" = engagement trap that generates likes)
- **Production cost:** 4-8 hours (initial template).
- **Key production elements:**
  - Animated counter/gauge synced to visual change
  - 3D or high-quality 2D rendering
  - Dark UI aesthetic (black + accent color)
  - Gamification hooks ("double-tap," "try to keep up")
  - Seamless loop where possible
- **Remotion feasibility:** HIGH — SVG path animation, counter components, glow/particle effects
- **Identifying signals:** Animated numbers/gauges, product UI elements, dark background, interactive prompt

### P3: Cultural Edutainment

- **Examples:** Calm "hurkle-durkle" (222K likes, 9.7M views), Oura "metsäterapia" (63.2K views)
- **Structure:** Dictionary-style definition card on premium footage (drone, cinematic nature)
  with a humorous or culturally relevant "see also" that bridges highbrow and internet culture
- **Viral mechanism:** Discovery + delight. Viewer learns an obscure/interesting concept, the
  "see also" makes them laugh, and the premium footage makes them feel sophisticated for
  engaging with it. The highbrow/lowbrow collision drives shares.
- **Production cost:** 1-2 hours.
- **Key production elements:**
  - Frosted glass card (semi-transparent, blurred background — borrowed from iOS design language)
  - Drone or cinematic stock footage (not AI-generated for this format — real footage reads as premium)
  - Clean typography with hierarchy (word, pronunciation, part of speech, definition, "see also")
  - Monochromatic palette aligned to footage (greens for nature, blues for ocean, etc.)
  - Slow camera movement (drift, pan) for contemplative feel
- **Remotion feasibility:** HIGH — backdrop-filter blur, text reveal animation, stock video background
- **Identifying signals:** Dictionary/definition card layout, frosted glass effect, drone footage, "see also" line

## The Taste-Virality Matrix

When classifying a Reel, place it on both axes:

```
                    HIGH VIRALITY
                         |
     T2 Quote Card       |      (sweet spot)
     T1 Meme Reel        |
                         |
LOW TASTE ───────────────┼──────────────── HIGH TASTE
                         |
                         |      P1 Functional Loop
                         |      P3 Cultural Edutainment
                         |
                    LOW VIRALITY
```

The goal for most brands is to move toward the upper-right quadrant: content that's both
high-taste and high-virality. The P3 Cultural Edutainment format is the closest proven
example — Calm's "hurkle-durkle" achieved 222K likes (high virality) with premium production
(high taste).

## Production Ladder

For accounts starting from near-zero followers, progress through tiers in this order:

**Phase 1 (0-1K followers):** T1 + T2 + P3 — maximize reach with minimum production, plus one premium format.
1 Reel/day. Test topics rapidly. T1/T2 under 8 seconds. P3 Cultural Edutainment (frosted glass + cinematic footage) differentiates early. The goal is algorithmic distribution.

**Phase 2 (1K-10K followers):** T3 + T4 — build identity and niche authority.
5 Reels/week. Develop visual consistency (color palette, typography). Start mixing in
P3 Cultural Edutainment to differentiate.

**Phase 3 (10K+ followers):** P1 + P2 + P3 — brand elevation.
2-3 premium Reels/week alongside T2-T4 content. Invest in Remotion templates, brand
illustration, and motion design system.
